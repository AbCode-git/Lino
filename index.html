About Lulit

Lulit is a certified makeup artist with a passion for bringing out the natural beauty in everyone. With years of experience in the beauty industry, she has mastered the art of transformation while maintaining the unique essence of each individual.

At LINO Beauty Lounge, we believe that beauty is an expression of self. Our approach combines technical expertise with artistic vision to create looks that not only enhance your features but also reflect your personality.

Located at Tropical Mall at Bole (next to Hiber Ethiopia), our studio provides a serene and elegant environment where you can relax and enjoy being pampered.
